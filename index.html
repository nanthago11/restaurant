A Cobb salad.

The Cobb salad is a main-dish American garden salad typically made with chopped salad greens (iceberg lettuce, watercress, endives and Romaine lettuce), tomato, crisp bacon, boiled, grilled or roasted (but not fried), hard-boiled eggs, avocado, chives, Roquefort cheese, and red-wine vinaigrette.
